Our Team

April White

Founder & Lead Agent

April White, born and raised in Yalobusha County, has been a resident of Lafayette County/Oxford, MS for over 31 years. With an in-depth knowledge of the area, April is passionate about real estate and approaches each day with enthusiasm for her work. A licensed real estate broker and owner of Right-Way Realty, she is committed to maintaining the company as a family-run business.
